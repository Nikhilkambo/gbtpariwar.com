GRAMIN BHARAT TRUST (NGO)

GBT - GRAMIN BHARAT TRUST (NGO) was established on 15th August 2019 by a body donator -Mr. OM PRAKASH THAKUR, Gramin Bharat Trust (NGO) is a non- governmental organisation. Its aim is to bring back the existence and importance of villages " चलो गाँव की और " which also means rural development. The aims and objectives of the trust are as follows:-

  • To spread awareness amongst public against the social evil for example :- poverty, unemployment, drug abuse, women empowerment etc...
  • Health problems is a major issue so for the improvement of health organizing yoga camps.
  • To focus on natural and organic farming, with latest technology.
  • To focus on cleanliness for saving the nature and promoting Swach Bharat Abhiyan.
  • To work for the upliftment of rural areas.
  • To set up blood banks and eye banks on the basis of voluntary donation
  • Under skill india program, providing professional skill developing courses to the students.
  • To organise cultural programmes in order to promote indian culture.
  • To provide educational facilities to the poor.
  • To establish libraries in village for the betterment of the poor kids who cannot afford new books .
  • To establish professional courses on agriculture and hospitality to develop the skills of the rural youth and remove unemployment as well.
  • To develop, construct, reconstruct, build, rebuild, maintain and alter the existing buildings for educational and charitable property and undertake religious and social activities at anywhere in india.
