GBT AGRO PVT LTD (COMPANY)

On January 2020, on the birthday of Swami Vivekanada and International Youth Day, Mr. Om Prakash Thakur the founder of Gramin Bharat Trust decided to form a foundation for setting up of the rural area development through Agro Tourism and Agro Trading activities , and to achieve this goal the company was formed as GBT AGRO PRIVATE LIMITED On January 28, 2020, the registration of the company was made from the ministry of corporate affairs.
  • The company will set up micro & small scale industries of Agro Trading & Agro Tourism in the rural areas.
  • The company will introduce natural and organic farming in the rural area with new technology for the youth of the rural areas.
  • Bringing tourists to the rural area will be arranged by the company for the purpose of rural tourism, photography and videography of the rural area according to the hospitality, lifestyle, Indian cuisine & pahadi culture.
  • Also tourists will get a chance to experience agriculture, horticulture and village culture.
  • To promote and provide Gramin destinations, resorts, home stays, camping sites, etc. Organise cultural and traditional festivals and activities, provide services like videography and photography with our traditional costumes and also provides tour packages for Gramin tourism, organise food fairs as well as traditional clothing or adventure fairs.
  • Marketing of hotels, resorts, governmental organisations will be done through banners and other advertisements and sales of different products as well as services will be done in agriculture and tourism.
  • To provide tour packages like adventure tour, cultural tour, historical tour, etc. All over India and for further increase our organisation we will take land, hotels, resorts, camping sites on lease in rural areas.
  • To provide transportation services through booking and modes of transport will be cabs, buses, trains and flights as per the demand and situation and transport facilities for farmers will also be provided to load and unload the products from their home to market, and many others.
