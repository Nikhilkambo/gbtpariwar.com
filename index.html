GBT PARIWAR GROUP

GBT PARIWAR Group is an upcomping organisation that will provide both social platform and business platform. GBT has two parts one is GRAMIN BHARAT TRUST (NGO) and the other is GRAMIN BHARAT TOURISM (COMPANY) when combined makes the GBT PARIWAR. Its motive is for youth , to make them self - reliant by providing a variety of programmes for rural development and rural upliftment . With this, the GBT AGRO PVT LTD (COMPANY) will provide new employment opportunities for the rural youth by setting up the Agro-Tourism and Agro-Trading organisation.
